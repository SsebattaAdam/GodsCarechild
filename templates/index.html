Learn More About Our Work And Our Cultural Activities

Welcome to God's Care Child Ministry Uganda, where we are dedicated to providing care and support to children in need within our community. Founded by Adam Ssebatta and his friends in Mityana, Uganda, our ministry began as a spontaneous response to the needs of children in SSekanyonyi, Mityana, Central Uganda.

At God's Care Child Ministry Uganda, our mission is to bring the Fatherhood of God to the fatherless through family structures, Christian education, and biblical self-sustainability. We aim to equip indigenous peoples to carry the message of Jesus, fostering a multi-cultural team focused on shared service.

We provide essential care and support to orphans, addressing the root causes of fatherlessness in Uganda. Our goal is to raise up a generation of godly men and women who will stand for truth, defend the widow, care for the orphan, and live out the Gospel of Jesus Christ in every area and sphere of life in Uganda.
